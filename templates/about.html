About Urexcore

Your trusted partner in verification & customer communication

Who We Are

Urexcore Private Limited operates as a premier third-party customer communication and verification service provider. We conduct high-volume outbound and inbound voice communications tailored for EV mobility companies, microfinance institutions, and women-focused loan programs.

Our expertise spans customer onboarding, lead verification, KYC confirmation, customer support, and service-related interactions. We are dedicated to supporting these business operations efficiently, ensuring scalable and reliable call handling capabilities that drive growth and customer satisfaction.
